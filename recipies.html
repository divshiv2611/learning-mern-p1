Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 T Wired Brain Coffee Hot Cocao Mix
  • 8 oz Milk
  • Mashmallows (Optional)

Steps

  1. Scoop hot cocoa mix into a mug
  2. Heat milk on the stove or in the microwave just before a boil
  3. Pour milk in to the mug, stirring gentaly as your pour
  4. Sitr until mix is fully dissolved into the milk
  5. Top with mashmallows if you must

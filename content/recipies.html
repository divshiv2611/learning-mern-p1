Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 T Wired Brain Coffee Hot Cocao Mix
  • 8 oz Milk
  • Mashmallows (Optional)

Steps

  1. Scoop hot cocoa mix into a mug
  2. Heat milk on the stove or in the microwave just before a boil
    1. Microwave on high for 30 seconds
  3. Pour milk in to the mug, stirring gentaly as your pour
  4. Sitr until mix is fully dissolved into the milk
  5. Top with mashmallows if you must

Cocoa Facts

Do you know the difference?
Cocoa
Cocoa comes from the seeds of the cacao tree (Theobroma cacao). Native to Central and South America, it was sacred to ancient cultures like the Maya. Cocoa is rich in antioxidants, boosts mood, and improves heart health. Leading producers are Côte d'Ivoire and Ghana. It's key in chocolate and skincare.
Chocolate
Chocolate is made from roasted and processed cocoa beans. It comes in various types—dark, milk, and white—based on cocoa content. Rich in antioxidants, dark chocolate offers health benefits, like improved heart and brain function. Enjoyed globally, it’s used in desserts, beverages, and snacks, with major producers in West Africa.

Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingrediants

  • 2 T Wired Brain Coffee Hot Cocoa Mix.
  • 8 oz Milk
  • Marshmallows

Steps

  1. Scoop Hot Cocoa Mix into a mug
  2. Heat milk on the stove or in the microwave to just below a boil
    1. Microwave on high for 30 seconds.
    2. Microwave on medium for 45 seconds.
    3. Microwave on low for 60 seconds.
  3. Pour milk into the mug, stiring gently as you pour.
  4. Stir until mix is fully dissolved in the milk.
  5. Top with marshmallows if you must.

Cocoa Facts

Do you know the difference?
Cocoa
Cocoa is the powdered substance left after processing cocoa beans and has little or no cocoa butter.
Chacolate
Chacolate is basically cocoa with the cocoa butter and ofetn sugar and milk.
